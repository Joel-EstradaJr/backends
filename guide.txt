FTMS Items PATCH guidance
-------------------------
- Prefer sending item-level pairs: `{ items: [{ receipt_id, item_id }] }`.
- This marks only the exact items that succeeded on your side.
- If you cannot include `receipt_id`, send `{ transaction_items: [{ transaction_id, item_id }] }`.
- The FTMS endpoint will resolve `receipt_id` internally and update the same items.
- Legacy `{ transaction_ids: string[] }` remains supported but is discouraged.

See `examples/external_ftms_items_route.ts` for a minimal client snippet.
